SharePoint Enterprise Wiki training hub


When this project was thought of, we didn't have much idea of what all this was going to be. Originally coined as a Wiki, we decided to focus on a resource library. As time moved forward, it was decided that a more user-friendly UX was needed.

During the development of this site, I spent a good majority of my time learning what would be our best use case. We spent a lot of time thinking of who the audience is and could be, while also trying to focus on how we can make this as scalable as possible.

Each training "module" links directly to the appropriate SharePoint library with a relative path. This allows for scalability in that changing the path will be as easy as changing the name to what is most-current.
