welcome to my home


This website will be used to house findings through data analysis and visualizations. My goal is to use this site and repository to build my vision of making data accessible and digestible to the world.

I believe that a world of united people is the direction we need to be moving in and we need to share that all people are equal as we share similar pain and sorrow. Just as equally, we share joy and love. Through this common understanding, we will lift the gates of trade and countries will flourish as we begin the journey to free ourselves from discrimination and xenophobia. All it takes is human empathy.

For now, we will work on speaking about mental health and hopefully find unbiased and real data to analyze regarding mental health patients.
