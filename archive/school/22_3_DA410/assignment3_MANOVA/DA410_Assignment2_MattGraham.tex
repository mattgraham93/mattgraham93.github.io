judges tasted fish samples and rated each on four variables: y1 ---
aroma, y2 = flavor, y3 = texture, and y4 --- moisture. The data are in
Table 6.17. Each entry is an average score for the judges on that fish.

\paragraph{Analysis}\label{analysis-1}}

According to our 4 tests, we can conclude there are differences among
our sowing dates across all measured variables. Variety, however, does
not have significance, which implies and is determined to be ineffective
at their cross product. This means sowing date and variety have no
difference among any variable. Extra simply, varieties are independent
of each other.
